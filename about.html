About Us

Who we are

African Media Hub is a live-streaming and video-on-demand mobile delivery platform specifically designed for the African Market. We work with global media giants and local broadcasters

Vision

Empower millions across Africa by providing affordable, accessible and relevant digital entertainment and educational content

Mission

To lead Africa’s digital entertainment sector by providing content through robust partnerships and social collaborations whilst ensuring a smooth user experience across various regions

What we do

  • Provide affordable, accessible digital content on a subscription basis tailored to African audiences
  • Over 125 channels, including VOD, sports, and educational content, are available on people’s smartphones
  • Engaging underserved markets with localised entertainment and education
  • PA low pricing strategy that enables access for everyone in society
